Design Brief and Site Content

Project Statement

Create a website for a family-owned restaurant, Hell Burger. It is a family-owned business serving quality classic American food. It also sells t-shirts, tote bags, and other accessories.

Objective

Provide a space to showcase Hell Burger’s menu, background/history, merchandise, and contact info.

Brief

Based on my research, websites for restaurants truly invest in high quality imagery to entice customers to try their food. They succeed at being memorable by exuding a unique brand personality, providing amazing customer service, and having a “user-friendly” website. Easy navigation and quick information are key. Additionally, because of the pandemic, more consumers are conscious about supporting local family-owned locations instead of chain restaurants.

My goal for this project is to create a dynamic website that has mouth-watering images, establish a fun/edgy personality for Hell Burger, and truly hone in on the fact that is it a “family-owned business.”

Target Audience

Young adults, 21 - 40 years old, who are passionate about supporting local businesses, love all-American food and are seeking a fun place to hangout/socialize.

Look and Feel

  • Edgy
  • Trendy
  • Unique
  • Eye-catching

Deliverables

  • Website
  • Restaurant Branding
  • Product Photography
  • Merchandise Mockups
